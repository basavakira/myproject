Dolo 650 MG Description

Dolo 650 tablet is a pain-relieving medicine. It also helps reduce fever. Dolo contains paracetamol as its active ingredient. This medicine is used for many conditions such as headache, backache, mig

raine, toothache, body pain etc. This medicine may be prescribed with other disease conditions such as typhoid or the common cold, to manage fever. Pain is an unpleasant feeling caused by the formation of certain chemicals in the body in response to an injury, tissue damage or disease. Dolo 650 mg tablet works by blocking the formation of these chemicals responsible for fever and pain. This medicine usually provides symptomatic relief to the patient taking this medicine. The dose or duration of this medicine varies among individuals depending on the severity and condition it is being used for. You should not take more than 4 tablets of the Dolo 650 in any 24 hours. Do not take it further if you feel that your pain and fever are relieved. This medicine is not meant for children under 12 years of age. Avoid taking Dolo 650 too often and for a prolonged duration without medical advice. Also, you should not take this medicine if you are allergic to paracetamol or any ingredient of the Dolo 650 tablet.

Uses of Dolo 650 MG

  • Fever
  • Tooth ache or tooth pain
  • Headache
  • Migraine
  • Backache
  • Body pain
  • Musculoskeletal pain

Contraindications of Dolo 650 MG

  • If you are allergic to paracetamol or any ingredient of the Dolo 650 tablet.
  • If you are taking any other product containing paracetamol.

Side effects of Dolo 650 MG

Chances of developing side effects with the Dolo 650 are rare if taken within the recommended doses. However, in rare cases, you may experience -
  • Skin rash
  • Itching
  • Blisters
  • Allergic reaction

Precautions and Warnings of Dolo 650 MG

Pregnancy

Q:
Can I take the Dolo 650 tablet during pregnancy?
A:
Dolo 650 can be used during pregnancy if the doctor recommends it. Yet, it should be used for a short duration in low doses and should not be used too frequently by pregnant women.

Breast Feeding

Q:
Can I take the Dolo 650 tablet while breastfeeding?
A:
The components of the Dolo 650 tablet pass into the breastmilk in a very small amount and has negligible effects on the breastfed baby. Thus, you can take it while you are breastfeeding.

Driving

Q:
Can I drive if I have consumed the Dolo 650 tablet?
A:
Dolo 650 tablet is not likely to affect your ability to drive or use machines.

Alcohol

Q:
Can I consume alcohol with the Dolo 650 tablet?
A:
Moderate consumption of alcohol when you are taking a Dolo 650 tablet is usually safe. However, there is a risk of liver damage in chronic alcoholics (people who drink too often or can not abstain from drinking) and people with liver problems.

Other General Warnings

Talk to your doctor if
  • You have severe liver or kidney disease or problems, seek medical advice before taking Dolo 650 mg tablet.
  • You are taking any other medicines or products that contain paracetamol. Taking too much of the same can cause liver damage.
  • You consume alcohol too often or are suffering from chronic malnutrition.
  • Dolo 650 tablet is not meant to be used in children below 12 years of age.
  • You should maintain a gap of 4-6 hours between each dose of the Dolo 650 and should not exceed it to more than 4 tablets a day.

Directions for Use of Dolo 650 MG

Take the Dolo 650 tablet as a whole with a glass of water or as directed by your doctor. Do not consume more than that recommended by your doctor.

Storage and disposal of Dolo 650 MG

  • Dolo 650 tablets should not be stored above 25°C.
  • Keep it out of the reach of children and pets.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can Dolo 650 be used for a headache?

A: Yes, Dolo 650 tablets can be used for headaches. However, if you are feeling you get the headache too often and there is not much relief in the headache after taking this medicine, consult your doctor.

Q: Can I give the Dolo 650 tablet to a five years old child?

A: No, Dolo 650 is not meant for use in children below 12 years of age.

Q: Is Dolo 650 safe during pregnancy?

A: It can be taken during pregnancy under medical supervision. A pregnant woman should avoid taking the Dolo 650 too often and for prolonged durations.

Q: Can I take the Dolo 650 for cold and fever?

A: You can take Dolo 650 tablet for controlling fever, it may not have any action in reducing cold as colds usually happen due to a viral infection.

Q: What is Dolo 650 tablet used for?

A: Dolo 650 mg is used to relieve fever and mild-to-moderate pain. It is also used to get relief from headaches, toothaches, migraine, period pain and other painful conditions.

Q: Dolo 650 vs Crocin, what are the differences?

A: Dolo 650 tablet contains paracetamol 650 mg and is generally used to treat fever and pain. Crocin tablet also contains paracetamol in different doses. Both medicines contain the same active ingredient, which is paracetamol.

Q: Any general warning I should be aware of about paracetamol?

A: Be cautious if you are a patient with liver and/or kidney-related conditions.

Q: Dolo 650 or paracetamol, which is better?

A: Dolo 650 contains paracetamol as an active ingredient and its pain-killer action is due to the presence of paracetamol in it.

Q: Meftal forte vs Dolo 650, What's the difference?

A: Meftal Forte Tablet is a combination medicine containing paracetamol and mefenamic acid as its active ingredient. Dolo 650 tablet contains only paracetamol. Both of these are pain-relieving medicine and are used to relieve mild to moderate pain and to reduce fever.

Q: Can I use the Dolo 650 for body pain?

A: Yes, the Dolo 650 tablet can be used to relieve body pain. Be sure to consult your doctor if your body ache is persistent and does not go away in a few days.

Q: Can the Dolo 650 be used for kids?

A: No, the Dolo 650 contains a high dose of paracetamol and it should not be used for kids below 12 years of age.

Q: Can we take the Dolo 650 after 4 hours?

A: Yes, the Dolo 650 tablets can be taken every 4 hours, but only after consulting a doctor.

Q: Does Dolo make you sleepy?

A: No, Dolo does not make you sleepy.

Q: How many days Dolo 650 can be taken?

A: You can take Dolo 650 regularly for many days as advised by the doctor, but don't take more than the recommended dose. Because overdosing on paracetamol can cause serious side effects.

Q: When should I not take Dolo 650?

A: You should not take Dolo 650 if you are allergic to paracetamol or any ingredient of the Dolo 650 tablet. You should be careful if you are taking any other product containing paracetamol. Also, if you notice any kind of allergic symptoms like skin rash, swelling and/or itching (especially on the face, lips, throat, etc.), dizziness, breathing difficulty, etc. after taking Dolo 650.

Q: Is paracetamol 650 and Dolo same?

A: Dolo 650 and paracetamol 650 are the name of the brands and contain 650 mg of paracetamol as their active ingredient, possessing the same benefit to the patient taking it.

Q: Is Dolo good for fever?

A: Dolo 650 is the most commonly prescribed medicine to control fever and pain relief. This medicine is used to treat body aches, headaches, toothache and other conditions also.

Q: Can I take Dolo 650 for a runny nose?

A: No, Dolo 650 is a pain-relieving medicine and should not be used for a runny nose.

Q: Can Dolo 650 be taken for chills?

A: No, Dolo 650 will not be effective in chills.
